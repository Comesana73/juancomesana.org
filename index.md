    <p>I am Professor and Director of Graduate Studies in the <a href="https://philosophy.rutgers.edu">Philosophy Department</a> at Rutgers.</p>
    <p>I specialize in Epistemology, but I am interested (and occasionally publish) in other areas as well.</p>

    <p>I was born in Argentina, and got my undergraduate degree in Philosophy at the [University of Buenos Aires](https://www.uba.ar/). After receiving my Ph.D. from [Brown](https://philosophy.brown.edu) in 2003, I taught at the [University of Wisconsin, Madison](http://philosophy.wisc.edu/) for six years. After that I joined the [University of Arizona](https://philosophy.arizona.edu) in the Fall of 2009, and moved to [Rutgers](https://philosophy.rutgers.edu) in 2022.</p>
