*About*

I was born in Argentina, and got my undergraduate degree in Philosophy at the [University of Buenos Aires](https://www.uba.ar/internacionales/contenido.php?id=455&lang=en). After receiving my Ph.D. from [Brown](http://www.brown.edu/Departments/Philosophy/) in 2003, I taught at the [University of Wisconsin, Madison](http://philosophy.wisc.edu/) for six years. After that I joined the [University of Arizona](https://philosophy.arizona.edu) in the Fall of 2009, and moved to [Rutgers](https://philosophy.rutgers.edu) in 2022.

You can download a copy of my [cv](/assets/documents/cv-latex.pdf).
